My Product Journey

From identifying customer problems to launching products that scale globally

It Started with a Customer Problem

My product journey began by observing real users struggle with emissions measurement. Working at a heating plant, I saw firsthand how organizations needed better tools to track, analyze, and reduce their carbon footprint. This customer empathy became the foundation of my product thinking — always start with the problem, not the solution.

Deep Customer Research

I invested years conducting user research and market validation — through atmospheric chemistry research and regulatory work, I learned how different stakeholders (scientists, engineers, compliance teams) approached environmental data. This taught me to design for diverse user personas and validate assumptions with data.

0→1 Product Launches
Enterprise Scale

Launching & Scaling Products

At Locus Technologies, I launched 4 carbon compliance products from 0→1 — defining product vision, conducting 58+ customer interviews, building roadmaps, and coordinating cross-functional teams. I learned to ship fast, iterate based on feedback, and drive adoption through customer success partnerships.

Now at UL Solutions, I own product strategy for AI-powered sustainability platforms serving Fortune 500 companies — managing product roadmaps, aligning stakeholders across engineering/sales/marketing, and driving business outcomes through data-driven decision making.

My product philosophy: Start with customer pain, validate with data, ship iteratively, and measure impact. The best products solve real problems for real people — everything else is noise.
